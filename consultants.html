What You Can Expect from Our IELTS Preparation Course

Grammar

Boost Your Grammar and Exam Awareness

We start by building a solid foundation with a focus on grammar and exam preparation strategies. Using helpful acronyms, you'll enhance your understanding of the IELTS exam and gain command over essential grammar skills, setting you up for success on your first attempt.

Reading

Mastering the Reading Section

In this module, we will dive deep into the reading section of the IELTS. Learn how the reading questions are structured, what types of questions to expect, and master strategies for tackling True, False, and Not Given questions.

Well also focus on the most challenging question type: Matching Headings. With free resources and proven strategies, you'll be well-equipped to handle even the trickiest parts of the reading exam!

Writing

Cracking Writing Task 2 (The Essay)

Writing Task 2 can be daunting, but not with our expert guidance! We will:

  • Highlight common mistakes and how to avoid them.
  • Break down the Official Marking Scheme, the key to scoring 8+.
  • Teach you quick strategies to plan and generate ideas for a Band 9 essay.
  • Walk you through writing opinion essays step-by-step, making this section easier to tackle.

Listening & Speaking

Enhance Your Listening and Speaking Skills

Our ultimate strategy for both the listening and speaking portions will elevate your skills to the next level. With personalized tips and targeted strategies, you'll feel confident handling these sections of the IELTS.

Ample free practice materials to ensure you're fully prepared for every aspect of the IELTS.
